# WitStartupPitch

## Register

Brand — the landing page is the primary surface; the product app will follow later.

## Users & Purpose

Early-stage founders preparing for demo day, investor coffees, or accelerator applications. They use WitStartupPitch at a kitchen table or co-working desk, often late at night, to turn scattered notes into a pitch investors can scan in minutes.

## Brand Personality

Honest · rehearsed · spotlight-ready

## Anti-References

- Dark violet SaaS hero with gradient cards
- Hero metric tiles (48h / 12+ / 0)
- Identical icon-card feature grids
- Generic "early access is free" gradient pricing band
- Uppercase tracked eyebrow on every section

## Strategic Principles

- Light, confident surfaces; brand color carries identity, not the background tint
- Typography does the heavy lifting — display sans + warm serif body
- Real imagery of founders pitching and collaborating
- Numbered steps only where order matters (the pitch workflow)
- Auth is embedded, not hidden — founders should sign in without hunting

## Accessibility

WCAG AA contrast minimum; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; semantic landmarks and focus states on all interactive elements.
